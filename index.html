FAQ – Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I place an order?
A: Click on your favorite design, and you’ll be redirected to our WhatsApp with the design pre-filled.

Q: What colors do you offer?
A: At the moment we are offering Black, white, Navy Blue, Grey and Olive green. Feel free to reach out to us if you need any specific color customization.

Q: What sizes do you offer?
A: All tees are oversized fits. They’re unisex and available in S, M, L, XL, XXL , XXXL. Size chart available on request.

Q: How long does delivery take?
A: We print on demand. Orders are usually delivered within 7-14 working days across Sri Lanka.

Q: What is your return policy?
A: We accept returns for damaged or incorrect items only.
